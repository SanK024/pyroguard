Low Voltage Systems Overview

At Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd, we specialize in a comprehensive range of low voltage systems designed to enhance safety, security, and efficiency in various environments. Our cutting-edge solutions include:

  • Gas Detection

    Protect your space with our advanced gas detection systems. Designed to monitor air quality and detect hazardous gases, these systems provide early warnings to ensure the safety of occupants.

  • Rodent Repellent

    Safeguard your premises from unwanted pests with our effective rodent repellent systems. Utilizing humane technology, these solutions deter rodents while maintaining a safe environment.

  • Water Leakage Detection

    Prevent costly damage with our water leakage detection systems. These intelligent sensors provide real-time alerts to potential leaks, allowing for prompt action and minimizing risks.

  • Addressable Emergency Evacuation Lights and Signage

    Ensure safety during emergencies with our addressable emergency evacuation lights and signage. These systems guide occupants swiftly and safely to exits, even in low visibility conditions.

  • Public Address Systems

    Enhance communication with our public address systems. Ideal for schools, offices, and public spaces, these systems deliver clear announcements and can be integrated for maximum efficiency.

  • CCTV Systems

    Secure your property with our state-of-the-art CCTV systems. Offering high-resolution video surveillance, these solutions provide real-time monitoring and recording for peace of mind.

  • Access Control

    Manage who enters your premises with our advanced access control systems. From key cards to biometric solutions, we provide customizable options to enhance security.

  • Integrated Systems (BMS)

    Optimize your building's performance with our Integrated Building Management Systems (BMS). Streamline the operation of all your low voltage systems for improved energy efficiency and monitoring.
