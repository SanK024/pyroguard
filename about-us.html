Our Story

At Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd., our mission is simple yet profound: to protect lives, property, and assets through cutting-edge fire protection solutions. We understand the critical importance of fire safety in both residential and commercial settings, and we are dedicated to delivering solutions that prioritize safety, efficiency, and reliability. Every project we undertake is driven by our commitment to providing unparalleled protection and peace of mind to our clients..

Our Mission

Founded in 1991, Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd. has established itself as a leading provider of fire protection solutions in India. With over three decades of experience, we have built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability in the fire safety industry. Located in the heart of New Delhi, our headquarters at S-79, Okhla Industrial Area, Phase-II, serves as the hub for our operations and customer service. Over the years, we have expanded our reach, serving a diverse clientele across various sectors including residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional.
