Our Story

Founded in 1991 by Mr. Pritish Tewari, Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd. has established itself as a leading provider of fire protection solutions in India. With over three decades of experience, we have built a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability in the fire safety industry. Our headquarters, located at S-79, Okhla Industrial Area, Phase-II in New Delhi, serves as the hub for our operations and customer service.

  • A Visionary Beginning

    The inception of Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd. was driven by Mr. Pritish Tewari's vision to create a safer environment through advanced fire protection solutions. With a keen understanding of the growing need for robust fire safety measures, Mr. Pritish Tewari set out to build a company that would not only meet but exceed industry standards.

  • Steady Growth and Expansion

    Over the years Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd.has expanded its reach, serving a diverse clientele across various sectors including residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional. Our growth is a testament to our ability to adapt to the evolving needs of our clients and the industry. We have successfully completed numerous projects across India, providing tailor-made fire protection solutions that cater to the unique requirements of each client.

  • Commitment to Innovation

    Innovation is at the heart of our operations. We continuously invest in research and development to stay at the forefront of fire protection technology. Our product portfolio includes state-of-the-art fire suppression systems, water mist systems, panel protection, and gas-based fire protection systems. By leveraging cutting-edge technology, we ensure that our solutions are not only effective but also efficient and environmentally friendly.

  • Excellence in Service

    Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our products to the services we offer. From initial consultation and design to installation and maintenance, we provide comprehensive support to ensure the optimal performance of our fire protection systems. Our team of highly skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and support, ensuring that our clients' safety needs are always met.

  • Client-Centric Approach

    At Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd.we place our clients at the center of everything we do. We understand that every project is unique, and we take the time to understand the specific needs and challenges of each client. Our customized solutions are designed to provide maximum protection while fitting seamlessly into the client's operational framework.

  • Industry Recognition

    Our commitment to quality and excellence has earned us recognition within the industry. We adhere to the highest standards of safety and quality, and our systems are compliant with all relevant industry regulations. Our reputation for reliability and innovation has made us a preferred partner for many prestigious projects across India.

  • Future Prospects

    Looking ahead, Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd.is poised for continued growth and innovation. We are committed to advancing the field of fire protection through ongoing research and development. Our goal is to set new benchmarks in the industry, ensuring that we provide the highest level of safety and protection for our clients.

  • Community and Sustainability

    Beyond our commitment to fire safety, Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd.is dedicated to giving back to the community and promoting sustainability. We engage in various corporate social responsibility initiatives, including educational programs on fire safety awareness and supporting local community projects. Additionally, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint by developing eco-friendly fire protection solutions and adopting sustainable business practices.

  • Leadership

    Under the visionary leadership of Mr. Pritish Tewari, Pyroguard Engineers Pvt. Ltd.has grown into a leading force in the fire protection industry. Mr. Tewari's expertise, dedication, and forward-thinking approach continue to drive the company towards greater heights, ensuring that we remain at the cutting edge of fire protection technology and service.
